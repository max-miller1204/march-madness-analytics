# Final Four Picks Assignment

## Submission Deadline

Submit your Final Four (Champions of each region) by **5 p.m. (BEFORE CLASS) on Wednesday, March 18**.

## Rules

For your Final Four, the sum of your seeds must be **15 or higher**.

That means, if you pick a No. 1 seed to make this year's Final Four, the other three teams must total at least 14: `1 + X + Y + Z = 15`. As long as your seeds total 15 or higher, you are good.

## Written Essay

You must also include a **written essay (200 words)** on why you picked each team to make the Final Four. Submit the essay on Canvas.

> **NOTE:** Your reasoning must be data-driven, not because "team X had the best player," etc. Submit your essay in a text box in Canvas.

## Bracket Upsets

The modern NCAA tournament (64/68 teams) has been around for 35 years. In 29 of those tournaments, there have been between 10 and 16 upsets (where the winning team was at least two seeds worse than the losing team). In all 36 years, there has been an average of **12.7 upsets**.

You should have at least **12 or 13 upsets** (on average) to get a decent grade on this bracket.
